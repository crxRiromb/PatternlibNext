Footer Example

The footer supports two modes: fixed and scrollable.

The fixed mode will keep the footer at the bottom of the page, while the scrollable mode will allow the footer to scroll with the page. The scollable footer is configured to show a Scroll to Top icon button on the right side. The settings for the footer is a global setting and will affect all pages.

Please note that the mode for Mobile Breakpoint is automatically set to scrollable.

You can toggle between the two modes by clicking the button below.
